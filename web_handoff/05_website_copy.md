## Tool cards
**Figures + statistics**
Upload your groups and values; NeuroFig picks the correct test (t-test, ANOVA, or a
non-parametric equivalent), runs it, and draws a clean journal figure — telling you
exactly which test it used.

**Dose-response (IC50 / EC50)**
Fit the four-parameter logistic to your dose-response data and get IC50/EC50 with a
confidence interval and the fitted curve — the analysis you'd open Prism for.

**Standard curve**
Fit protein-assay, qPCR, or ELISA standards and back-calculate your unknown samples.
qPCR curves report amplification efficiency automatically.

**Time-course (ΔF/F)**
Plot calcium/photometry traces with SEM shading and compare two conditions with a
cluster-based permutation test — the statistically correct way to compare curves.

**20 journal figure types**
Volcano, Manhattan, raincloud, swimmer, Circos, Sankey, treemap, split violin,
heatmaps and more — all colourblind-safe and export-ready.

**PDB → PDBQT**
Clean a protein or ligand structure and convert it for AutoDock/Vina docking, with
correct partial charges — the tedious prep step, done right.

**Bench calculators**
Molarity, dilutions, DNA copy number, primer and protein properties, dosing, and
more — every result checked against known reference values.
